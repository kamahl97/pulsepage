# 15-Minute Weekly Site Health Routine

Do this once a week before sending traffic, posting offers, or running ads.

## Minute 0-3: mobile check

- Open homepage on phone.
- Open main product/service page.
- Tap the main call-to-action.

## Minute 3-6: money path check

- Start checkout, booking, or contact flow.
- Confirm button text and price are correct.
- Confirm confirmation page/message appears.

## Minute 6-9: trust check

- Are hours, prices, links, and contact info current?
- Do reviews/testimonials/portfolio items still show?
- Is there anything that looks abandoned?

## Minute 9-12: speed and broken stuff

- Scroll the page.
- Look for broken images, weird layout, slow loading, popups covering buttons.

## Minute 12-15: log it

Record:

- Date checked
- What worked
- What broke
- Fix needed
- Next check date
