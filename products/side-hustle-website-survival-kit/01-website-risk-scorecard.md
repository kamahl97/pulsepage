# Website Risk Scorecard

Use this once a week. Score 1 point for every YES.

## Money page basics

1. My homepage loads on phone and desktop.
2. My main offer/product/service page loads.
3. My checkout, booking, order, or contact path works.
4. My contact form sends to the right email.
5. My business phone/email/social link is visible.
6. My SSL padlock is active and not expired.
7. My site loads in under 4 seconds on mobile data.
8. My most important button is visible without scrolling.
9. My refund/cancellation/contact expectations are clear.
10. My site has no obvious broken images or broken links.

## Trust signals

11. Visitors can tell what I sell within 5 seconds.
12. Prices or next-step instructions are clear.
13. There is at least one trust signal: reviews, portfolio, guarantee, credentials, real photos, or clear business details.
14. The site does not look abandoned: current year, working links, updated hours, active inventory/services.
15. Customers know what happens after they pay, submit, or book.

## Monitoring

16. I have a monitor watching the main website.
17. I have a monitor watching checkout/contact/booking.
18. I know who gets alerted if something breaks.
19. I have checked the site from my phone in the last 7 days.
20. I have a simple outage plan.

## Score

- 17-20: Strong. Keep monitoring.
- 13-16: Good, but there are leaks to close.
- 8-12: Risky. You may be losing sales quietly.
- 0-7: Emergency mode. Fix the basics before sending traffic.
