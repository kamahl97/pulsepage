# Customer Trust Quick Fixes

These are small fixes that often matter more than fancy design.

## Make it obvious

- What do you sell?
- Who is it for?
- What does it cost?
- What should the visitor do next?

## Add trust

- Real business name
- Contact email
- Clear refund/cancellation policy
- Reviews, screenshots, case studies, portfolio, before/after examples
- Delivery timeline or service expectations

## Remove doubt

- Replace vague buttons like “Submit” with “Get quote” or “Book appointment.”
- Explain what happens after form submission.
- Put the main CTA near the top.
- Make phone/email tappable on mobile.
- Remove dead social links.

## The 5-second test

Show your page to someone for 5 seconds. Ask:

1. What is this business selling?
2. Who is it for?
3. What should I click next?

If they cannot answer, the page is leaking money.
