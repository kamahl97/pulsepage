# Side Hustle Website Survival Kit

A practical digital download for solo founders, freelancers, Etsy sellers, Shopify sellers, creators, and small business owners who rely on one website, store, landing page, booking page, or checkout page to make money.

Promise: help non-technical owners catch preventable website money leaks before customers do.

Suggested price: $9 launch price, then $19.

## Included files

- `01-website-risk-scorecard.md` — score your site/store in 10 minutes.
- `02-money-page-monitoring-checklist.md` — what to monitor and how often.
- `03-outage-response-script.md` — what to do when the site, checkout, booking, or contact form breaks.
- `04-weekly-site-health-routine.md` — 15-minute weekly routine.
- `05-customer-trust-quick-fixes.md` — fast fixes that increase confidence.
- `06-launch-day-checklist.md` — pre-launch/relaunch checklist.
- `07-tool-stack-under-50.md` — low-budget stack.
- `08-owner-dashboard-template.csv` — simple tracking sheet.
- `gumroad-listing-copy.md` — product listing copy.

## Positioning

This product is intentionally not a generic “make money online” guide. It sells a concrete fear: losing sales because a money page quietly broke.
