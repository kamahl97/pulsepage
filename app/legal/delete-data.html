Data Deletion Request

Last updated: May 11, 2026

PulsePage stores monitor data such as monitor names, website URLs or server targets, uptime check results, response times, SSL status, and alert history so the app can provide uptime monitoring.

Request deletion

To request deletion of your PulsePage monitor data, email us at support@getpulsepage.com with the subject line PulsePage data deletion request.

What to include

  • Your name or contact email.
  • The monitor name, website URL, or server target you want deleted.
  • Any details that help us identify the data.

What we delete

We will delete monitor records, stored check history, and related alert history associated with the request when we can verify the data belongs to you or your account/test setup.

What may be retained

Basic security, abuse-prevention, or operational logs may be retained for a limited time when needed to protect the service, comply with legal obligations, or troubleshoot system reliability.

Response time

During the MVP/testing phase, we aim to respond to deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe after receiving enough information to identify the relevant data.
