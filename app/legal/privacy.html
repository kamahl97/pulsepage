Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

PulsePage is a simple website and server uptime monitoring app. This policy explains what information the app uses and why.

Information we collect

  • Monitor names and targets you add, such as website URLs or server/port targets.
  • Uptime check results, response times, SSL certificate status, and alert history.
  • Basic technical logs needed to keep the service reliable and secure.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect contacts, photos, microphone data, location, or advertising identifiers.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not use third-party advertising inside the app.

How information is used

We use monitor data to check whether your websites or servers are reachable, show current status, maintain recent history, and provide alerts or reports.

Data storage

PulsePage stores monitor and check data on our backend server. Admin actions require a private token during this early testing phase.

Data deletion

During the MVP/testing phase, contact support to request deletion of your monitor data. In-app deletion/export flows are planned before public launch.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact: support@getpulsepage.com
