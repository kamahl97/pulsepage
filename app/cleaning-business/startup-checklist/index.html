Beginner checklist

Cleaning business startup checklist

A practical first-pass checklist for starting lean and avoiding expensive overthinking.

Before your first job

  • Choose one starter service.
  • Write what is included and what costs extra.
  • Create a basic supply list.
  • Set a minimum job price.
  • Prepare a quote message.
  • Prepare a payment method.
  • Track each job in a spreadsheet or notebook.

Do not buy too much too early

Beginners often waste money on branding, tools, or supplies before proving they can land and complete jobs profitably. Start simple.

What matters most

Show up, communicate clearly, quote carefully, do good work, track the numbers, and learn from each job.
