Cleaning Business Money Kit
Gumroad: https://kamahl97.gumroad.com/l/cleaning-business-money-kit
Price: $9 validation launch
Audience: solo cleaners and new cleaning business owners

## Strategy

No calls. No cold DMs. No spam.

Use public useful content:

1. Free checklist page ranks/gets shared.
2. Helpful posts point to free checklist.
3. Free checklist points to Gumroad product.
4. SEO guides point to both checklist and Gumroad.

## 14-day scorecard

Track:

- Landing page visits
- Free checklist visits
- Gumroad visits
- Sales
- Conversion rate
- Which post/page drove traffic

Decision after 14 days:

- Traffic low: distribution problem.
- Traffic high, no sales: listing/offer problem.
- Sales happen: make more cleaning-business tools around the same audience.
