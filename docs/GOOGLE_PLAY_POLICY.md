# Google Play Policy Guardrails

We will keep PulsePage boring-clean for approval.

## We will do
- Provide real functionality, not a webview clone or spam app.
- Use Google Play Billing for Pro upgrades.
- Clearly disclose subscriptions and paid limits.
- Collect minimal data: account info, monitor URLs, push token, diagnostics.
- Publish a privacy policy before store submission.
- Use HTTPS for backend traffic.
- Avoid unnecessary permissions.

## We will not do
- No contacts/SMS/location/camera/microphone permissions.
- No fake virus/security claims.
- No gambling, contests, cash rewards, crypto, or financial advice.
- No deceptive ads or disruptive full-screen ads.
- No scraping private user accounts.
- No spammy store listing keywords.

## Data Safety draft
Data collected:
- Email/account identifier, if login is added.
- Website URLs the user asks us to monitor.
- Device push token for alerts, if notifications are added.
- Diagnostics/crash logs.

Data not collected:
- Contacts
- SMS/call logs
- Location
- Photos/files
- Health data
