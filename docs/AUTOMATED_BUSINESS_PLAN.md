# Automated Business Plan: No Calls / No DMs / $50 Budget

Created: 2026-05-12

## Randy's constraints

- Budget: about $50.
- No sales calls.
- No cold DMs.
- Low people-dealing.
- Automation-first.
- Tom should build and operate as much as possible.
- Goal: create income, starting with first-dollar validation.

## Reality check

No online business is truly passive at the start. The build can be automated heavily, but distribution is still the hard part. The best fit is a self-serve product that people can find, understand, buy, and download without a conversation.

## Ranked options

### 1. Free niche tool + paid digital upsell

Build a useful free page that attracts search/social/community traffic, then sell a small downloadable kit or template.

Examples:

- Website risk scorecard
- Broken money-page checklist
- Shopify/store audit checklist
- Contractor quote calculator
- Freelancer rate calculator
- Website launch checklist

Why it fits:

- Tom can build almost everything.
- No calls or DMs required.
- Can run on existing PulsePage site/VPS.
- Can sell through Gumroad/Ko-fi/Stripe Payment Link.
- Low budget.

Main risk: traffic/discovery.

### 2. Etsy/Gumroad digital downloads

Sell templates/checklists/planners/spreadsheets.

Why it fits:

- No inventory.
- No calls.
- Built-in payment/download flow.

Main risk: very crowded. Generic products will die. Needs a specific niche and good listing SEO.

### 3. Shopify app / store audit micro-tool

Build a scanner that identifies revenue leaks on Shopify stores.

Why it fits:

- Store owners already care about money.
- Shopify App Store has buyer intent.
- Shopify's developer revenue-share terms are favorable for small apps.

Main risk: app approval and support burden.

### 4. Android apps

PulsePage is already in motion, but Google Play production access is blocked by 12 testers + 14 days.

Why it fits later:

- App can run self-serve.
- Subscriptions/ads possible.

Main risk: slow discovery and current Play testing blocker.

### 5. Affiliate/content site

Useful long-term, poor immediate-income path.

Main risk: SEO can take months.

## Decision

Start with option #1 using the PulsePage niche:

**Side Hustle Website Survival Kit**

A $9 digital download for side hustlers, freelancers, creators, and small business owners who rely on one website/checkout/contact page to make money.

Positioning:

> Stop losing sales to silent website problems.

## Why this is the first product

- Reuses PulsePage branding and domain.
- Directly connects to the app we already built.
- Can be delivered as a ZIP.
- No support calls required.
- Can later upsell PulsePage monitoring.
- Can be launched before Play production access.

## What has been built

- Product files under `products/side-hustle-website-survival-kit/`.
- Download ZIP under `app/download/side-hustle-website-survival-kit.zip`.
- Landing page under `app/products/side-hustle-website-survival-kit/index.html`.
- Gumroad listing copy under product folder.

## Next steps

1. Create a Gumroad or Ko-fi seller account.
2. Upload the ZIP.
3. Set launch price to $9.
4. Add payment link to the landing page.
5. Publish/deploy landing page to getpulsepage.com.
6. Create 5-10 SEO/free-tool pages around money-page checks.
7. Post publicly in non-DM channels only: Reddit posts where allowed, Indie Hackers/Product Hunt-style launch, personal/social posts, GitHub README links.

## Budget use

- $0: product creation and hosting on existing VPS.
- $0: Gumroad/Ko-fi storefront to start.
- $0-$10: optional graphics if needed.
- $0-$25: reserved for Google Play / future validation.
- Avoid paid ads until checkout/contact path is proven.
